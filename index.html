My Services

My Services

We offer individual and group therapy and psychological support for both women and men facing challenges, as well as for first responders


Woman's Trauma Therapy

Offering individual or group therapy for persons who identify as female and who may or may not struggle with substance abuse or addictive behavior.

Trauma-focused therapy for survivors of any traumatic events including childhood, sexual, domestic, trafficking, and narcissistic trauma.

Individuals will have the opportunity to process their trauma and examine how it has led to maladaptive coping skills and self-loathing.

The goal will be to break these patterns and beliefs while transitioning to self-care, self-forgiveness and self-love.


Men's Trauma Therapy

Offering individual or group therapy for persons who identify as male and who may rely on substances and/or addictive behavior to cope with their trauma.

Trauma events can include childhood abuse, sexual trauma, loss of job/status, divorce, religious trauma, or any event that you have found to be traumatic for you.

Individuals will be provided a safe space to process their trauma and may for the first time, share what happened to them.


First Responders / Military / Veterans

Recognizing the need for discretion and privacy in meeting the needs of these special individuals, specialized groups have been tailored for first responders and military personnel.

These groups will process the firsthand and secondary trauma they have experienced and explore healthier coping mechanisms beyond alcohol and substances while healing among their peers.

These individuals can also be seen individually as well as with their partners and/or family members.

This is an opportunity to address how the demands of these career paths can take a toll in general on mental health and family dynamics along with ways to improve and be proactive in managing stress and maintaining overall mental health.
